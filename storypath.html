Your Learning Journey

A story of Gabriel — and you

Step into Gabriel's shoes across three chapters of school life, making choices that shape your path through an interactive story about learning, curiosity, and self-discovery.

Every decision reveals something about how you think and learn.

How to Play: You are Gabriel. As you move through school, you will face moments where you need to make choices — about how to learn, who to work with, and what matters to you.

There are no wrong answers. Some paths will be harder, some more rewarding. Pay attention to why you make each choice. That's where the real learning happens.

A Note for Players

Throughout this game, you were never asked to get the "right" answer. You were asked to make choices — and to notice, even briefly, why you made them.

That noticing is the point. It's what Gabriel was learning to do, across every scene. And it's what self-directed learning actually looks like: not a perfect plan, but a growing awareness of yourself as someone who learns, adapts, reflects, and keeps going.

What kind of learner are you becoming?
